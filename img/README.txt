Add the following images to this folder:

1. profile.jpg - Your profile/avatar image (recommended size: 400x400px)
2. project1.jpg - Image for your first project (recommended size: 800x600px)
3. project2.jpg - Image for your second project (recommended size: 800x600px)
4. project3.jpg - Image for your third project (recommended size: 800x600px)
5. hero-bg.jpg - Background image for the hero section (recommended size: 1920x1080px)

You can use free stock photos from websites like:
- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
- Pexels (https://pexels.com)
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)
